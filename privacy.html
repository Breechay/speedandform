Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 24, 2026

FORM is a running training app. This policy explains what information the app collects, how it is stored, and your rights as a user. Your training data is stored on your device and in your personal iCloud account. FORM does not operate servers that store your data, and does not sell or share your personal data with third parties.

FORM does not transmit your personal data to Speed and Form servers. All training data is stored on your device and, if you are signed into iCloud, in your personal iCloud account — which only you can access. FORM does not sell or share your personal data with third parties.

01

Information We Collect

FORM collects only the information necessary to provide the app experience. This includes:

Account information. When you sign in with Apple, Apple provides FORM with a unique user identifier and, if you choose, your name. FORM stores this identifier locally on your device to maintain your session. We do not receive or store your Apple ID email address unless you explicitly choose to share it.

Training data. Your training log entries, session history, Ghost Protocol progress, cycle records, paces, skip reasons, and onboarding preferences are stored on your device using Apple's native storage systems. If you are signed into iCloud on your device, this data also syncs to your personal iCloud account so it is available across your devices and persists if you reinstall the app. See Section 03 for more detail.

Notification preferences. If you grant permission, FORM schedules local notifications on your device (Tuesday threshold reminders and Saturday long run reminders). These are generated and delivered entirely on-device. No notification data is sent to any server.

Diagnostic data. FORM uses Apple's MetricKit framework to receive crash and performance diagnostic reports. This data is written to your device's local Documents folder only. MetricKit data is provided by Apple and governed by Apple's own privacy practices.

02

Information We Do Not Collect

FORM does not collect, access, or transmit the following:

We do not access Apple Health or HealthKit data. We do not use third-party analytics services (such as Firebase, Mixpanel, or similar). We do not use advertising networks or tracking identifiers. We do not collect location data. We do not access your contacts, camera, microphone, or photo library. We do not track you across apps or websites owned by other companies.

03

How Your Data Is Stored

FORM stores your training data in two places, both of which are under your control:

On your device. All data is saved to your device's local storage using Apple's native systems. This data is available immediately and works without an internet connection.

In your iCloud account. If you are signed into iCloud on your device, FORM also writes your training data to iCloud Key-Value Storage — a feature of Apple's iCloud platform that syncs data across your own devices and preserves it if you reinstall the app. This data lives in your personal iCloud account and is not accessible to Speed and Form. It is governed by Apple's iCloud privacy policy.

You can manage or disable iCloud sync at any time through your device's Settings → Apple ID → iCloud. Disabling iCloud for FORM will stop syncing but will not delete data already stored on your device.

04

Sign in with Apple

FORM uses Sign in with Apple as its authentication method. Apple's privacy-preserving login system means Apple authenticates your identity — FORM does not receive your Apple ID credentials or password.

You may choose to hide your email from FORM when signing in. If you do, Apple provides a private relay address that is not stored or used by FORM.

05

Deleting Your Account

You can delete your FORM account at any time from within the app. Deleting your account permanently removes all locally stored data and revokes your Sign in with Apple credential for FORM. This action cannot be undone.

To also remove data stored in iCloud, go to Settings → Apple ID → iCloud → Manage Account Storage and remove FORM's stored data from there.

06

Children's Privacy

FORM is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided information through the app, please contact us and we will take steps to remove it.

07

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of FORM after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. For material changes, we will make reasonable efforts to notify users through the app.

08

Third-Party Services

FORM integrates only with Apple's native frameworks: Sign in with Apple, iCloud Key-Value Storage, UserNotifications, and MetricKit. Each of these is governed by Apple's own privacy policy, available at apple.com/legal/privacy.
