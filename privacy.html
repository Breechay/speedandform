Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 23, 2026

FORM is a running training app. This policy explains what information the app collects, how it is stored, and your rights as a user. Your training data stays on your device. FORM does not sell or share your personal data with third parties.

FORM does not transmit your personal data to our servers. All training data is stored locally on your device using Apple's native storage systems. FORM does not sell or share your personal data with third parties.

01

Information We Collect

FORM collects only the information necessary to provide the app experience. This includes:

Account information. When you sign in with Apple, Apple provides FORM with a unique user identifier and, if you choose, your name. FORM stores this identifier locally on your device to maintain your session. We do not receive or store your Apple ID email address unless you explicitly choose to share it.

Training data. Your training log entries, Ghost Protocol progress, session history, and onboarding preferences are stored locally on your device using Apple's native storage systems. This data does not leave your device.

Notification preferences. If you grant permission, FORM schedules local notifications on your device (Tuesday threshold reminders and Saturday long run reminders). These are generated and delivered entirely on-device. No notification data is sent to any server.

Diagnostic data. FORM uses Apple's MetricKit framework to receive crash and performance diagnostic reports. This data is written to your device's local Documents folder only. MetricKit data is provided by Apple and governed by Apple's own privacy practices.

02

Information We Do Not Collect

FORM does not collect, access, or transmit the following:

We do not access Apple Health or HealthKit data. We do not use third-party analytics services (such as Firebase, Mixpanel, or similar). We do not use advertising networks or tracking identifiers. We do not collect location data. We do not access your contacts, camera, microphone, or photo library.

03

How Your Data Is Stored

All FORM data is stored locally on your device using Apple's native storage systems. Data is stored only for as long as the app is installed on your device.

If your device is backed up to iCloud, your FORM data may be included in that backup under Apple's standard iCloud backup policies. This is governed by your iCloud settings and Apple's privacy policy, not by FORM.

04

Sign in with Apple

FORM uses Sign in with Apple as its authentication method. Apple's privacy-preserving login system means Apple authenticates your identity — FORM does not receive your Apple ID credentials or password.

You may choose to hide your email from FORM when signing in. If you do, Apple provides a private relay address that is not stored or used by FORM.

05

Deleting Your Account

You can delete your FORM account at any time from within the app. Deleting your account permanently removes all locally stored data and revokes your Sign in with Apple credential for FORM. This action cannot be undone.

06

Children's Privacy

FORM is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided information through the app, please contact us and we will take steps to remove it.

07

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of FORM after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. For material changes, we will make reasonable efforts to notify users through the app.

08

Third-Party Services

FORM currently integrates only with Apple's native frameworks (Sign in with Apple, UserNotifications, MetricKit). Each of these is governed by Apple's own privacy policy, available at apple.com/legal/privacy.
