Common questions
Account deletion
Open the FORM app → Profile → scroll to Account → Delete account. All local training data is removed immediately. If you no longer have access to the app, email us with the subject line "Delete Account" and we will remove your data within 72 hours.
Ghost Protocol vs. FORM mode
Ghost Protocol is a six-week movement foundation for new runners. FORM mode is the weekly training structure — threshold Tuesday, long run Saturday — for experienced runners. You can switch between them anytime in Profile.
Strava and run detection
FORM can detect completed runs via Strava to close your training loop automatically. Connect or disconnect in Profile → Run Detection. FORM does not post to Strava on your behalf.
Metronome and background audio
The metronome continues playing when the screen locks or the app is backgrounded. Set the BPM, start it before your run, lock the screen. The click stays on. If audio stops after a phone call, open the app and restart the metronome.
Feedback and feature requests
Email support@speedandform.com. Include what you were doing, what you expected, and what happened instead.
