What Not To Do
—
Don't turn Tuesday into a performance. The 400s are about removing resistance to speed, not proving you have it. Run them quick and loose, not fast and hard.
—
Don't force Thursday into a test. The 2 × 2 miles should feel almost easy. If it feels like you're proving something, you missed. The goal is calibration, not confirmation.
—
Don't add anything because you "don't feel ready." Apr 6–9 you may feel heavy, flat, disconnected. This is not fatigue — this is organization happening without stimulation. Do nothing.
—
Don't go out at 5:52 because you feel great. Key Biscayne is flat. The energy at the start will lie. First 3 miles held back, every time.
