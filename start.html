FORM Start

Start

A practice of disciplined distance running.

FORM is a structured training group based in Miami. Two sessions anchor the week — threshold on Tuesday, long run on Saturday. Everything else supports those two sessions.

This is not a social run club. It is a training environment. Sessions are planned in advance. The structure does not change to accommodate mood or novelty. The repetition is the point.

The Three Sessions Where the week is organized
Thursday
Speed 5:50 AM · Flamingo Park Track · the easiest entry Short reps with full recovery between. You run the distance and see what the watch says. No pace band required. The track is the equalizer — anyone can run 200 meters.
Tuesday
Threshold 5:50 AM · Flamingo Park Track Sustained aerobic effort at a strong but controlled intensity. Warm-up is part of the session. The group begins together.
Saturday
Long Run 5:30 AM · Hideout, Edgewater Easy early. Steady later. Finish neutral. The durability anchor of the week.
System How FORM Training Works

Three ideas organize training: easy running, threshold work, and long run durability. Each session develops a different part of the system. Speed is not chased directly — it emerges from structure.

How to Begin First session protocol
00 Download the FORM app. It carries every session, the coaching layer, and your ledger. New to structured running? Start with Ghost Protocol — six weeks of mechanical practice before group sessions.
01 Start with Thursday. Short reps, full recovery between each, no specific pace required. Show up at Flamingo Park Track at 5:50 AM. That's the whole instruction.
02 Run your effort, not the group's. If someone goes ahead, let them go. The work is individual, done in parallel.
03 Finish neutral. Do not empty the tank in the first session. Leave something unused. The system rewards runners who manage effort across weeks, not sessions.
04 Check the practice board before each session. It shows the current week — what is scheduled, what phase the group is in.
05 When Thursday feels like something you want more of — Tuesday and Saturday are the rest of the practice.
