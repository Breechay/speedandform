Training Phases
Organization Build the habit. Easy running, structural work, and a weekly threshold session. Volume is modest. The goal is consistency, not intensity.
Density More of the same work, held longer. Threshold reps lengthen. Long runs extend. The week asks for more repetition at quality.
Compression Threshold sessions are fuller. Long runs have more sustained steady work. Volume approaches its ceiling. Efficiency matters more than effort.
Expression Speed work appears. Volume drops. The base built over previous phases becomes the foundation for faster running.
Absorption After a race or hard block. Volume drops significantly. Easy running and structural work only. The body consolidates what was built.
